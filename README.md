# GenFortune 🥠

An onchain fortune cookie dApp built on GenLayer.

## What it does

Pay 0.1 GEN. An Intelligent Contract calls an LLM. Multiple AI validators reach consensus on your unique fortune. Result is final onchain.

## Why only possible on GenLayer

The fortune doesn't exist until you open the cookie. No hardcoded list. Multiple validators running different AI models independently generate and compare results via the Equivalence Principle. Pure decentralized AI consensus.

## Rarity tiers

- ◇ NORMAL — a truth you needed to hear
- ◆ RARE — something that stops you mid-scroll  
- ◈ UNIQUE — a message that feels written for you alone
- ✦ LEGENDARY — the oracle chose you. 1 in 100.
